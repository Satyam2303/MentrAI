About

MentrAI is built for institutions that want scalable personalization without losing instructional control. Created by AI researchers and educators with firsthand experience in high-stakes learning, the platform replaces fragmented tools with a single workspace for teaching, communication, and assessment.

How it Works

A consistent flow for institutions to deliver curriculum, personalize practice, and measure mastery.

  1. 01

    Upload syllabus inputs

    Add chapter outlines or learning objectives to ground lessons to the cohort curriculum.

  2. 02

    Generate structured content

    Produce coherent lessons, explanations, solved examples, and checkpoints that match classroom pace.

  3. 03

    Personalize practice and assessment

    Create adaptive assessments and question banks that target each student’s weaknesses and build measurable mastery.

  4. 04

    Intervene with clarity

    Use cohort insights to spot at-risk students, content gaps, and pacing issues, then assign targeted interventions.

Infrastructure

Custom-trained AI agents and fine-tuned open-source models, aligned to curriculum, approved textbooks, and teacher inputs.

Curriculum grounded generation

Content generation is constrained to institution-approved material to improve coherence and reduce off-syllabus output.

Long-form consistency

Produces textbook-length content with stable structure, diagrams, tables, visualizations, and worked solutions.

Rubric-based evaluation

Supports structured feedback on written work and adaptive assessments that map to learning outcomes.

Cohort-level insights

Identify at-risk students, concept bottlenecks, and pacing issues, then act with targeted, trackable interventions.
